About TECHMAX Tours & Safaris

Your trusted partner for unforgettable Zanzibar adventures

Who We Are

TECHMAX Tours & Safaris is a premier tourism company dedicated to showcasing the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Zanzibar. Founded with a passion for adventure and a commitment to sustainable tourism, we have been crafting unforgettable experiences for travelers from around the world since our inception.

Our team of experienced guides and travel experts work tirelessly to ensure that every journey is not just a trip, but a transformative experience that connects you deeply with the wonders of Zanzibar's pristine beaches, vibrant history, and diverse wildlife.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, personalized travel experiences that showcase Zanzibar's natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventure opportunities while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading tourism company in Zanzibar, recognized globally for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and responsible travel that creates lasting positive impacts on both visitors and the local environment.

What We Offer

Guided Tours

Explore Zanzibar's iconic sites with expert guides, from Stone Town's historic streets to wildlife reserves.

Adventure Packages

Tailored packages for all types of travelers, including honeymoons, groups, and luxury adventures.

Excursions

Day trips and special activities like snorkeling, spice farm tours, and cultural performances.

Car Rental

Reliable vehicle rentals for self-guided explorations across Zanzibar's diverse landscapes.

Mbweni Apartment

A modern and peaceful apartment located in the serene neighborhood of Mbweni. Perfect for guests seeking comfort, privacy, and quick access to the beach and airport.

Kilimani Apartment 1

A stylish and fully furnished apartment in Kilimani, offering quick access to Stone Town, shopping centers, and major attractions. Ideal for couples and business travelers.

Kilimani Apartment 2

A cozy and spacious apartment designed for families or long-stay travelers. Located in a secure area with great accessibility to transport, restaurants, and beaches.
