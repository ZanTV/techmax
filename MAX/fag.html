Cancellations & Rescheduling

Cancellations made 30 days or more before the tour date receive a full refund. Between 14–29 days, a 50% refund applies. Less than 14 days, unfortunately, no refund is possible.

Yes! Tours can be rescheduled up to 14 days before departure, subject to a small rescheduling fee. Contact us to arrange.

Travel Information

Bring comfortable clothing, sun protection (hat & sunscreen), water, personal medications, and suitable footwear. For beach activities, include swimwear and towels.

Zanzibar is generally safe. Stick to tourist-friendly areas, use registered taxis, and follow local guidance for a worry-free experience.

The ideal period is June to October (dry season) for sunny weather and fewer crowds. Avoid April–May, which is the rainy season.
