Our Policies

Clear guidelines for your safe and enjoyable Zanzibar experience

TECHMAX Tours & Safaris Policies

Privacy Policy

At TECHMAX Tours & Safaris, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

  • Information Collection: We collect information you provide when booking tours, contacting us, or subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Use of Information: Your data is used to process bookings, provide services, and send relevant updates about your trips.
  • Data Protection: We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.
  • Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties without your consent.

Terms of Service

By using our services, you agree to these terms. Please read them carefully before booking any tours or services.

  • Acceptance: Booking with us constitutes acceptance of these terms and conditions.
  • Services: We provide tour guiding, transportation, accommodation booking, and related services in Zanzibar.
  • Liability: While we strive for safety, participants assume inherent risks of adventure travel.
  • Changes: We reserve the right to modify services due to weather, safety, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Booking Policy

Learn about our booking procedures to ensure a smooth reservation process for your Zanzibar adventure.

  • Reservation: Bookings can be made online, by phone, or email. Confirmation is sent within 24 hours.
  • Payment: Full payment is required at booking for most services. We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments.
  • Confirmation: You'll receive a booking confirmation with all details and what to bring.
  • Group Bookings: Special arrangements available for groups of 10 or more with advance notice.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Understand our cancellation terms to plan your trip with confidence.

  • Cancellation by Client: Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive 90% refund. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund.
  • Cancellation by TECHMAX: Full refund or alternative arrangements offered if we cancel due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • Force Majeure: No refunds for cancellations due to natural disasters, political unrest, or pandemics.
  • Refunds: Processed within 14 business days after cancellation approval.

Liability & Safety Policy

Your safety is our priority. Read about our commitment to responsible tourism and risk management.

  • Safety Measures: All tours include experienced guides and safety briefings. Equipment is regularly maintained.
  • Health Requirements: Travelers are responsible for their health and any required vaccinations or medical preparations.
  • Insurance: We recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost baggage.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We follow sustainable practices to minimize our impact on Zanzibar's ecosystems.

Group Tour Policy

Special guidelines for our group tours and packages to ensure everyone has an enjoyable experience.

  • Group Size: Tours accommodate 2-20 participants for optimal experience and safety.
  • Punctuality: Please arrive 15 minutes early for all tour departures to avoid missing activities.
  • Behavior: Respect local customs, guides' instructions, and fellow travelers for a positive group dynamic.
  • Special Needs: Inform us of any special requirements or accessibility needs when booking.

Sustainability Policy

Our commitment to preserving Zanzibar's natural beauty and supporting local communities.

  • Eco-Friendly Practices: We use low-impact transportation and support conservation initiatives.
  • Local Support: We partner with local businesses and employ community members to maximize economic benefits.
  • Waste Management: All tours include guidelines for responsible waste disposal and minimal environmental impact.
  • Education: We educate travelers about Zanzibar's culture and encourage respectful interactions with locals.

If you have any questions about these policies, please contact us.
